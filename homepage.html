Samiti

The work of Vidyarthi Sahayyak Samiti is seen as an organization that transforms the lives of thousands of students in rural areas and as a center for youth transformation. The Committee is a body registered under Section 50A (3) of the Public Trustee Act, 1950 (No. E-219, Pune) with the Office of the Charity Commissioner and has jurisdiction over the whole of India. Students who are financially weak in the society but who are striving to achieve something with the strength of quality can get accommodation and food in Pune at a very modest priceFor the purpose of providing facilities, senior mathematician Dr. Achutrao Apte, freedom fighters Haribhau Phatak, Sumitratai Kerkar etc started this work in 1955.

Activity

The work of the committee is not only limited to providing accommodation and food at low cost, but the main goal of the committee is the personality development of the students here. Various programs are organized for this purpose. The spirit of the committee is to transform these rural youths into well-educated and self-confident generation through student management.Group Discussion, English Language Class. English presentation, yoga, sports (compulsory), cultural programs (organization of competitions in cookery, debate, writing, storytelling etc.), participation of experts in various fields.

facilities

Samiti means not only Lodging and Boarding but accommodation here at reasonable rates along with food arrangements The concept of personality development is of utmost importance. History


Dr. The concept of Vidyarthi Sahaya was planted in 1955 from the concept of Achyut Shankar Apte, a great social worker. While studying in France, he experienced that the citizens and the government help the needy students for their education. This brought to his attention the country's approach to education and reflected on the state of education in India at that time. After returning to India, talking to like-minded people, five rural students were initially provided with food in Pune. The construction of hostels with accommodation and food arrangements for the needy in the rural areas and for the aspiring students who want to prove themselves by coming to Pune for higher education was realized from this concept. The name of this concept is 'Vidyarthi Sahayyak Samiti'.
